FAQ
Questions? Answered.

Do I need an app to use TapKaro?

No. The receiver doesn't need any app. They just hold their phone near your card and the profile opens automatically in their browser. Works on iPhone XS+ and all modern Androids.

What if their phone doesn't have NFC?

Every TapKaro card includes a QR code on the back. They can scan it with their camera for the same experience. 100% of smartphones covered.

Can I update my info after ordering?

Yes, unlimited times, forever, for free. Your card points to a digital profile that you can edit anytime. New job, new number — just update online.

How long does the card last?

The NFC chip has a 10-year data retention and can be read hundreds of thousands of times. The PVC card itself is waterproof and highly durable.

How long does delivery take?

5-7 business days from order confirmation. We design your card, set up your digital profile, program the NFC chip, and ship it to your door.

Can I order in bulk for my team?

Absolutely. We offer 10-30% discounts on bulk orders of 5+ cards. Contact us for custom corporate pricing with branded designs.
